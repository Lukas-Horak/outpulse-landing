Features

Everything you need to scale outbound

From prospect discovery to personalized sending. One platform, no duct tape.

AI Prospect Discovery

Search millions of verified contacts to find decision-makers who match your ideal customer profile. Filter by role, industry, company size, tech stack, and funding stage — all powered by real-time data from Apollo's database.

Hyper-Personalized Emails

Every email is unique — not a template. AI uses prospect data like role, company, tech stack, and funding to write messages that feel genuinely hand-written.

Smart Campaigns

Multi-step email sequences with automatic follow-ups. Auto-pause on reply. Track opens, clicks, and responses in real time.

Intent Signal Detection

Know when prospects are ready to buy — hiring signals, funding rounds, tech changes, and company growth detected automatically.

Website Intelligence & ICP Generation

Paste your URL and AI reads your entire site, understands your offer, and auto-generates your ideal customer profile. No manual setup — just results in seconds. Your ICP drives discovery, personalization, and campaign targeting across the entire platform.
