### Short description (260 chars)

### Full description

**Most tools help you send emails. Outpulse helps you hit your revenue goal.**

You have a number in mind — $10K MRR, 50 new clients, $500K in consulting revenue. But between that goal and today, there's a gap. How many website visits do you actually need? How many meetings? How many emails? And who should you even be reaching out to?

Outpulse answers all of that — and then does it for you.

**Pulse: your growth command center**

Tell Outpulse your revenue target and whether you sell a product or a service. Pulse reverse-calculates your entire funnel:

→ For products: visits needed → signups → activated users → paying customers
→ For services: visits needed → replies → calls → proposals → closed deals

Then it connects to your Google Analytics, tracks real progress every day, and sends you a morning email: here's where you stand, here's what's working, here's what to do if you're falling behind.

It's like having a growth advisor that actually knows your numbers.

**AI outreach on full autopilot**

Pulse knows how many prospects you need. Outpulse goes and gets them:

1. **Finds your ideal prospects** — Describe your target client. AI searches and matches companies and decision-makers.
2. **Writes hyper-personalized emails** — Every message is crafted based on the prospect's company, role, and context. No templates.
3. **Sends multi-step sequences** — Automated follow-ups timed for maximum reply rates.
4. **Detects and routes replies** — Positive replies surface instantly. You only step in to close.
5. **Self-improves over time** — Campaigns with low reply rates get automatically rewritten by AI. No manual A/B testing.

**Who is Outpulse for?**

Solo founders, freelancers, small agencies, and lean sales teams who don't have time to manually prospect, write, and follow up — but need a predictable pipeline to grow.

Whether you sell a $29/mo SaaS or $50K consulting engagements, Outpulse adapts to your price point and distribution model. Inspired by Peter Thiel's framework from Zero to One: the way you sell should match what you sell.

**Start in 2 minutes. No CRM required.**

Connect your email. Describe your ideal client. Set your goal. Outpulse handles the rest.

**Pricing:**
Free to start. Growth plan at $149/mo for unlimited campaigns, full AI optimization, and Pulse.
