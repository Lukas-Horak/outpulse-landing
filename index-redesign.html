FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a free trial?
Yes — every plan starts with a 7-day free trial. You can test the full platform: AI prospect discovery, email generation, and campaign launching. No charge until the trial ends, and you can cancel in one click.
How does the AI write personalized emails?
Our AI analyses each prospect's real data: their role, company, tech stack, recent funding, hiring activity and more. It writes a unique email for each person — no templates, no mail-merge.
Will my emails land in spam?
Outpulse includes built-in email warm-up that gradually increases your sending volume over 14 days. Combined with SPF, DKIM and DMARC verification, your emails are optimized for deliverability from day one.
Where does the prospect data come from?
We use an enterprise-grade verified database of 275M+ contacts. All data is GDPR compliant and regularly updated — verified emails, job titles, company info, tech stacks, and buying intent signals.
Can I connect my existing email?
Yes — Outpulse integrates with Gmail and Outlook. Emails are sent from your real address, building trust and improving deliverability.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime from your account settings.
