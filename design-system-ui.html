01 · Foundations

Colour

Ink on cream is the default. Brand accents (orange, violet, blue, green) are reserved for signal — italics, active states, outcomes, and data categories in the dashboard. Never more than two accent colours at once on a given screen.

Typography

Instrument Serif is the display voice — headlines, KPI numbers, the one italic accent on a page. Inter runs the working layer (UI, body, metadata). JetBrains Mono for tokens and values. Never mix serif and sans in the same line.

03 · Foundations

Spacing, radius, and elevation

Increments of 4 for everything inside components; increments of 8 between components. Radius scales up as the container gets larger. Elevation is used sparingly — the product is flat, shadows are reserved for cards that really do lift.

05 · Components

Buttons

Three button levels: primary (ink, one per page), ghost (neutral), and danger. Three sizes — sm / md / lg — plus two specialised variants: icon button (square 36px) and FAB (56px circle, bottom-right of mobile). Primary hovers to ember to make the click feel earned.

06 · Components

Forms

Inputs sit on paper with a 1px line and a 3px ember focus ring. Labels are tiny uppercase grey; never inline placeholders in place of labels. Checkboxes and toggles follow the same ink-on-focus convention.

07 · Components

Badges + chips

All small. All uppercase-friendly. The beta badge is the only interactive one (click it — it's earned). Goal chips flip colour based on campaign type. Signal chips carry a coloured dot for the trigger category.

10 · Components

Overlays + feedback

AI insights sit in a calm violet-to-cream wash so they read as suggestion, not interruption. Toasts are ink pills, bottom-centre. Empty states get a subtle icon + two-line message — never a big splash.
